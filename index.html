About Gabriel

Who I am & how I work

I’m a branding-focused business consultant who enjoys helping people with good products look and sound as good as they actually are.

My work combines strategy + visual clarity. That means we don’t just make things look “nice” — we make your brand easier to understand, easier to remember and easier to choose.

I prefer working closely with founders and small teams, understanding their story, and building a brand that feels honest, simple and a little bit premium.
